Research

Three threads, one atmosphere from fair-weather fields to runaway electrons.

Magdeburg, Germany

Fair-weather atmospheric potential gradient

Building and analysing a multi-year PG dataset from a suburban CS110 field mill, data-quality corrections, diurnal & seasonal climatology, and comparison with the global Carnegie curve. Paper in preparation.

Mt. Aragats, Armenia

Thunderstorm ground enhancements & terminating lightning

High-energy atmospheric physics: gamma-ray and electron fluxes during thunderstorms, and the peak currents of the lightning flashes that abruptly terminate them (GLD360 / ENTLN, 2017–2023). Paper in preparation.

Southwest Asia

Lightning climatology & weather radar

Regional lightning climatology from ~435 million ENTLN flashes combined with satellite observations and reanalysis, plus CAPPI processing of MRL-5 weather-radar data for thunderstorm case studies.
